How Our System Works

We help professionals and aspiring professionals to connect with clients who are seeking true talent.

For Job Seekers

This is where we allow professionals and aspiring professionals to showcase their talent both on paper and through live video. All job seekers will upload a two-page resume, complete a behavioral questionnaire and a 90 second live video to articulate their soft skills which are often not identified until much further into the interview screening process. This saves time, energy, effort and money for both the potential candidate and potential employer.
